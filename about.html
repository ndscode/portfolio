Hi, I'm Ned Marafawi!



I enjoy solving problems with design and code that require me to work outside of my comfort zone.

My goal with coding is to turn ideas into reality and solve real-world problems, and improve the lives of people around the world.

With my myriad experience, I have failed a lot of times and because of those experiences, it made me a stronger person and forced me to re-evaluate my decisions, and my way of doing things. This is why, I'm constantly on the lookout to learn and experience new things.

Moreover, I strongly believe that constant learning and dedication are key elements for success. By taking lessons out of other's experiences has helped me tremendously. With all of these combined, I consider myself to be responsible, self-motivated, hardworking, ambitious and creative developer.

In other words, I'm always sharing my experiences through social media, so someone else can learn from them.

Finally, when I'm not coding, I find myself spending quality time with friends and family. Aside from that, I also enjoy playing video games and watch Twitch Tv.
